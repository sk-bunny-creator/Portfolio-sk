Every project starts with a single spark of an idea. For me, that spark is often a question: "What if?" What if an AI could dream? What if a machine could paint with emotion? This portfolio is a collection of those questions, explored through the lens of generative art and creative code.

Chapter 1: The Spark

This is where I explore the initial concept. I gather inspiration, sketch out ideas, and define the creative boundaries. It's a process of discovery, of finding the narrative within the noise.

Chapter 2: The Code

Here, I translate the concept into a tangible form. I write the algorithms, train the models, and craft the prompts that will bring the vision to life. It's a delicate dance between logic and art.

Chapter 3: The Creation

Finally, the AI and I collaborate. The machine generates, and I curate. It's a partnership that results in something new, something unexpected, something that neither of us could have created alone.
